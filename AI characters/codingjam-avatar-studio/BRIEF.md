# Week 2: AI Avatar Generator (MMV)

## 1. The Contract (I/O)

**Input:** A photo (user's face or a pet's face) and a choice of 1 of 4 style presets (e.g., Pixar, anime).

**Output:** A single stylized avatar image.

## 2. The Magical Moment

Pure delight and shareability ("My cat looks like a Pixar character!").

## 3. Scope Discipline (What we are NOT building)

One avatar at a time. No bulk generation, animations, character lore, or auto-formatted social media sizes.

## 4. Personality / Signature Detail

Deciding the strict input rules (Faces only? Full body?) to shape the app's specific focus and soul.
